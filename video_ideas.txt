# Hook (opening image + motion prompt)

hook_image_prompt = A raw, unfiltered eye-level mobile phone photograph showing the entrance of an old rural animal shelter during cold late autumn. A rusty metal gate stands half open. Sitting directly in front of it is a small orange-and-white rescue cat with messy fur and a faded blue collar. Next to the cat lies a worn dog toy rope with bite marks. In the background, inside the fenced yard, a young Golden Retriever puppy is being carried away by a family toward a car. The puppy is looking back toward the cat. Flat cloudy daylight, muted colors, slightly desaturated, realistic dirt textures, no cinematic grading, iPhone realism, vertical 9:16.

hook_video_prompt = Static handheld camera, no zoom, no camera movement, slight natural handheld shake only. Filmed on iPhone. Animate the scene naturally. The family slowly walks away carrying the puppy while the orange-white cat remains motionless near the gate. The puppy looks back and whimpers softly. Wind moves dry leaves across the ground. No music. Important sound effects: puppy crying softly, distant shelter ambience, wind.
