[IDEA]

name = The Warrior And The Arrow

# Hook (opening image + motion prompt)

hook_image_prompt = A raw, dramatic eye-level image of a lone ancient warrior standing motionless in the middle of a battlefield at dawn. Arrows are frozen mid-air around him, inches away from impact. Broken shields, dust, fallen banners, smoke in the distance. The warrior remains calm, eyes forward, hand resting on his sword but not drawing it. Cold blue dawn light, realistic textures, cinematic realism, vertical 9:16.

hook_video_prompt = Static camera with slight handheld movement only. Time appears slowed. Dust moves through the air. Arrows slowly pass by the warrior but he does not react. Cloth and hair move in the wind. Deep battlefield ambience. No music. Distant metal sounds and wind.

# Grid 1 (story panel 1 — uses Hook image as reference)

grid1_image_prompt = A 2x3 grid of six cinematic action panels with the same warrior.

Panel 1: Young warrior training alone in heavy rain while others rest.

Panel 2: Enemy soldiers mocking him while he remains silent.

Panel 3: Warrior carrying stones uphill alone at sunrise.

Panel 4: Close-up — someone insults him face to face. He does not react.

Panel 5: Battlefield preparation. Others panic while he calmly sharpens his sword.

Panel 6: Return to battlefield. The warrior stands still as arrows fly.

Atmosphere: realism, dust, sweat, grit, muted colors, cinematic stoic energy.

grid1_video_prompt = Animate each panel separately.

1: Heavy rain while warrior trains continuously.

2: Soldiers laugh and point; warrior stays still.

3: He carries stones uphill with effort.

4: Angry man shouting inches from his face; warrior remains calm.

5: Soldiers rush chaotically while he sharpens his blade.

6: Battlefield arrows pass around him in slow motion.

# Grid 2 (story panel 2 — uses Grid 1 image as reference)

grid2_image_prompt = A 2x3 action finale grid.

Panel 1: Enemy army charging full speed.

Panel 2: Everyone around him begins retreating.

Panel 3: Warrior takes one calm breath and steps forward alone.

Panel 4: Close-up of determined eyes.

Panel 5: He raises his sword and leads the counterattack.

Panel 6: Sunset aftermath. Battlefield quiet. Warrior sits alone cleaning his sword while others celebrate behind him.

Atmosphere: dust, motion, realism, stoic strength.

grid2_video_prompt = Animate each panel.

1: Massive charge toward camera.

2: Soldiers running back in fear.

3: Warrior calmly steps forward.

4: Wind moves hair and cloak.

5: Fast action charge sequence.

6: Silent sunset ending while he cleans the blade.

# Narration (34 seconds)

“Everyone wants strength…

But nobody wants the rain.

Nobody wants the insults.

Nobody wants the lonely mornings.

The Stoics understood something most people never will…

You do not become strong in battle.

You become strong before it.

In silence.

In discipline.

In moments nobody sees.

Because when chaos finally arrives…

The trained mind does not panic.

It steps forward.”
